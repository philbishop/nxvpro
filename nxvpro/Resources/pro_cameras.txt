<b>Cameras

Shows a list of cameras and NVRs that are not assigned to any groups.

If the cameras associated with an NVR are also discoverable you can move them to the OTHER DEVICES group via the camera login screen.

<b>Groups

Shows a list of your camera groups.

NVRs have groups automatically created with their associated cameras

<b>Map

Shows a list of all your cameras

Tap on an item to view or set the camera's location
